Programmable Soft Matter

From Proteins to Hierarchical Fibers

Impossible Fibers is building a universal fiber fabricator: leveraging advances in protein design, soft-matter physics, microfluidics, and fiber spinning we are creating the next-generation manufacturing technology that can transform billion dollar industries.

What We Do

We are developing new spinning pathways and molecular assembly mechanisms inspired by biology—from vesicle-based dopes to multi-phase coacervates—and pair them with advanced characterization and manufaturing modeling to bridge sequence → structure → performance.

Our work spans lab-scale rigs, digital-twin modeling, and collaborations with partners around the world to advance fiber technology that we believe will result in highly dextrous robotics and biomedical devices, advanced sensing and responsive textiles, and computational fibers that offer novel AI capabilities.

Collaborate with Us

We partner with researchers, industrial teams, and funding organizations interested in the future of protein-based fibers, soft robotics, and bio-inspired manufacturing.
