Careers — Laboratory Associate

Laboratory Associate - Impossible Fibers Lab

Impossible Fibers is a pioneering R&D lab building the next era of fiber manufacturing. Our bio-inspired approach provides unique control of fiber assembly from the molecular to the macro scale, enabling advanced technologies across robotics, computation, aerospace, and textiles.

As Impossible Fibers grows, we're building an exceptional team spanning engineering, chemistry, and materials science. We're currently seeking a Laboratory Technician to work closely with leads in engineering, materials, and prototyping to execute experiments and support daily lab operations. Your work will directly impact our ability to produce and improve breakthrough manufacturing strategies that transform industries. You'll gain hands-on experience in a fast-paced R&D environment while contributing to technology that revolutionizes manufacturing systems.

Responsibilities

  • Support microfabrication processes including micro-milling, 3D printing, and process troubleshooting
  • Perform established solvation protocols for proteins, supporting regular dope formation
  • Conduct quality control and standard characterization of dopes, fibers, and films
  • Collaborate with team leads to execute projects
  • Document all processes for open publication
  • Be a creative team player

Ideal Background

The ideal candidate likely has a mix of chemical or mechanical engineering, bioengineering, materials science, applied physics, protein chemistry, or related fields with interest or experience in:

  • CAD and 3D printing
  • Microfluidics
  • Protein characterization or bio-mineralization
  • Microscopy and materials characterization (e.g., AFM, optical, SEM, rheology)

The candidate should demonstrate strong written and verbal communication skills, high ownership and initiative, and a passion for discovery, innovation, or bio-inspired manufacturing.

Details

- Location: Emeryville, CA (on-site, full time)
- Compensation: $65-75k salary + benefits

Impossible Fibers Lab is part of ASTERA Institute and is dedicated to providing a supportive work environment for all our employees. We provide a competitive salary and benefits to join this cutting-edge research and development team.
